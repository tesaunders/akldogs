# Auckland Council Animal Management dog data

Data relating to the Auckland dog population taken from Auckland Council
Animal Management annual reports.

## Usage

``` r
dogs
```

## Format

A dataframe with 12 rows and 6 columns:

- year:

  Financial year data was reported

- dogs:

  Known dog population

- registered:

  Number of known dogs with a current registration

- desexed:

  Number of known dogs that have been desexed

- menacing:

  Number of known dogs classified as menacing under the Dog Control Act
  1996

- dangerous:

  Number of known dogs classified as dangerous under the Dog Control Act
  1996
