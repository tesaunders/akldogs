# Auckland Council Animal Management service data

Data relating to requests for service from Auckland Council Animal
Management annual reports.

## Usage

``` r
service
```

## Format

A dataframe with 12 rows and 23 columns:

- year:

  Financial year data was reported

- p1_central:

  Priority 1 requests for service completed in the central region

- p1_north:

  Priority 1 requests for service completed in the northern region

- p1_south:

  Priority 1 requests for service completed in the southern region

- p1_west:

  Priority 1 requests for service completed in the western region

- p1_east:

  Priority 1 requests for service completed in the eastern region

- p1_other:

  Priority 1 requests for service completed in other regions

- p2_central:

  Priority 2 requests for service completed in the central region

- p2_north:

  Priority 2 requests for service completed in the northern region

- p2_south:

  Priority 2 requests for service completed in the southern region

- p2_west:

  Priority 2 requests for service completed in the western region

- p2_east:

  Priority 2 requests for service completed in the eastern region

- p2_other:

  Priority 2 requests for service completed in other regions

- agg_animals:

  Requests relating to dogs being aggressive to animals

- agg_people:

  Requests relating to dogs being aggressive to people

- attack_animals:

  Requests relating to a dog attack against an animal

- attack_people:

  Requests relating to a dog attack against a person

- barking:

  Requests relating to a dog barking

- bylaw_breach:

  Requests relating to a bylaw breach

- pickup_dog:

  Requests relating to picking up a contained dog

- roaming:

  Requests relating to a roaming dog

- unregistered_dog:

  Requests relating to an unregistered dog

- welfare:

  Requests relating to a welfare check on a dog
