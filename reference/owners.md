# Auckland Council Animal Management dog ownership data

Data relating to dog owners from Auckland Council Animal Management
annual reports.

## Usage

``` r
owners
```

## Format

A dataframe with 12 rows and 5 columns:

- year:

  Financial year data was reported

- owners:

  Total number of dog owners

- disqualified:

  Total number of disqualified dog owners

- probationary:

  Total number of probationary dog owners

- rdol:

  Total number of dog owners with a Responsible Dog Owners Licence
  (RDOL)
