# Auckland Council Animal Management enforcement data

Data relating to Auckland Council Animal Management enforcement
activities taken from Auckland Council Animal Management annual reports.

## Usage

``` r
enforcement
```

## Format

A dataframe with 12 rows and 9 columns:

- year:

  Financial year data was reported

- prosecutions:

  Total prosecutions or appeals

- infringements:

  Total infringements issued

- failure_bylaw:

  Infringements issued under s20(5) - Failure to comply with any bylaw
  authorised by the section

- failure_control:

  Infringements issued under s53(1) - Failure to keep dog under control

- failure_disqual:

  Infringements issued under s53(1) - Failure to comply with the effects
  of disqualification

- failure_menacing:

  Infringements issued under s33EC(1) - Failure to comply with effects
  of classification of dog as menacing dog

- failure_register:

  Infringements issued under s42 - Failure to register dog

- obstruction_officer:

  Infringements issued under s18 - Wilful obstruction of dog control
  officer or ranger
